🍔 Food Delivery Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive **Food Delivery Analysis Dashboard** built using Power BI.
It analyzes customer orders, delivery performance, restaurant ratings, and revenue trends to generate meaningful business insights.

---

 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze food delivery trends across different cities
* Evaluate restaurant performance based on ratings and revenue
* Understand customer behavior and preferences
* Identify key factors affecting delivery efficiency

---

 📊 Dashboard Pages

 🟢 Page 1: Overview Dashboard

* Total Orders, Revenue, Avg Delivery Time, Avg Rating
* Orders by City
* Orders Trend (Monthly)
* Payment Method Distribution

---

 🟡 Page 2: Performance Analysis

* Avg Rating by Restaurant
* Total Revenue by Restaurant
* Orders by Food Category
* Orders by City
* Interactive filters for deeper analysis

---

 🔵 Page 3: Insights Dashboard

* Orders Trend Over Time
* Delivery Time vs Rating (Scatter Analysis)
* Category-wise Performance
* Top vs Low Performing Restaurants
* Key Business Insights

---

 📂 Dataset Details

* Total Records: 1000+
* Columns Used:

  * Order_ID
  * Order_Date
  * City
  * Restaurant_Name
  * Food_Category
  * Order_Value
  * Delivery_Time
  * Rating
  * Payment_Method
  * Order_Status

---

 ⚙️ Tools & Technologies

* Power BI
* Microsoft Excel
* Data Cleaning & Transformation

---

 🔍 Key Insights

* Fast Food and Desserts are the most popular categories
* Faster delivery generally results in better ratings
* Some cities have higher order volume and demand
* Top-performing restaurants maintain consistent ratings
* Cancelled orders impact overall service performance

---

 🚀 Features

* Interactive slicers (City, Category, Restaurant, Date)
* Dynamic KPI cards
* Clean and professional UI design
* Real-time filtering and insights
